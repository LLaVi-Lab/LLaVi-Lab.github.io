AI Seminar - Spring 2025

The UNT Artificial Intelligence (AI) seminar aims to cover a broad variety of AI topics, such as computer vision, natural language processing, machine learning, AI fairness & ethics, multimodal learning, etc. The goal is to facilitate research discussion and collaboration in AI.

Time: Unless otherwise noted, the events are on Mondays at 11:00am-12:00pm (every week).

Room: Unless otherwise noted, we will meet at Discover Park F219 in person (see the schedule on Google sheet for each event).

Who can attend? All students (PhD/MS/Undergraduate/K-12 students) who are interested in AI are welcome to attend our seminar.
